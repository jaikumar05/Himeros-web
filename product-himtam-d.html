Product Information

Description

HIMTAM-D is a dual-action formulation containing Tamsulosin Hydrochloride PR and Dutasteride, designed for the effective management of Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH) and Lower Urinary Tract Symptoms (LUTS). It provides both rapid symptom relief and long-term prostate size reduction, helping improve urinary flow and overall quality of life.

Key Benefits

  • Provides relief from urinary difficulties associated with BPH
  • Improves urine flow and reduces urinary obstruction
  • Helps reduce prostate enlargement over time
  • Supports long-term management of BPH progression
  • Lowers the risk of acute urinary retention
  • Helps alleviate Lower Urinary Tract Symptoms (LUTS)

Usage & Dosage

Usage: Management of Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH) and Lower Urinary Tract Symptoms (LUTS).

Dosage: As directed by your physician.

Side Effects

Generally well tolerated when used as prescribed. Consult your healthcare professional if any unwanted effects occur.
